Technologies

Project Overview

A collaborative effort to conceptualize the impact of COVID-19 across all U.S. states by synthesizing data from over 10 medical and federal databases. I played a role in developing, publishing, and presenting detailed dashboards that analyzed each state's response to the pandemic. These dashboards were then presented to 50 U.S. state governors, providing them with comprehensive insights to inform their decision-making and policy development regarding the ongoing crisis.
